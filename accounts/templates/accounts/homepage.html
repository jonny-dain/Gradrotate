How It Works...

Gradrotate assigns interns to roles that align with their abilities and potential for growth, while also bringing value to the employer. The system carefully matches each intern with the appropriate placement in 3 steps:

Job Creation
Managers Create A Vacancy Listing With Skills Needed For The Job

Intern Collection
Interns Create A Personality Profile And Rank Each Job In Order Of Preference

Allocation
Interns and Jobs Are Matched Together Based On Both Parties Preferences

Frequently Asked Questions...

Interns are recommended Jobs based on the 'Personality Profile' they complete. A Jaccard Weighting Coefficient is calculated between the Job and Intern, that compares various factors such as: skills, preferred office location, preferred remote/in-person balance, etc. Interns are then presented with jobs that have a higher coefficient value first, along with a suitable fit percentage.

Jobs are given preferences over interns based on their 'Personality Profile', rather than relying on managers to select interns. This approach significantly reduces unconscious bias in the selection process, ensuring that interns are evaluated objectively for their fit with a given role. To achieve this, we use an advanced calculation that compares the job profile against the intern's profile, resulting in a suitability ranking that guides our selection process.

The Intern-Job allocation calculation depends on the system requirements and can be calculated using various allocation algorithms. These algorithms differ in their approach of calculating pairs: some algorithms only require the intern's preferences, while others require both the job and intern's preferences. Regardless of the algorithm used, the intern's preferences always receive priority, and job preferences are only considered when a trade-off occurs.

We strive to allocate interns to their placement positions in a fair and equitable manner, based on their preferences and skills. However, in the unlikely event that an intern is not satisfied with their job placement, we encourage them to inform their supervisor. Our team will work diligently to address their concerns and find a resolution that meets their needs. Our ultimate goal is to ensure that every intern is content with their placement and finds their role engaging and fulfilling.
